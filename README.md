Align is a mobile workout notification app that centralizes workout scheduling, sharing, and tracking into one interface. The goal of this app is to encourage friends to stay active and hold accountability by making workouts more social and visible among friend groups. Specifically, Align helps users plan workouts with friends, get notified about upcoming sessions, share completed workouts socially, and track streaks and stats for workouts done together.
 
Many people struggle to maintain staying active when exercising alone, and existing fitness apps often focus only on individual tracking. Align’s concept is interesting because it encourages people to spend time with their friends in a way that also supports their physical health. Instead of socializing in mostly sedentary settings, this app promotes physically active meetups, where friends connect by working out or moving their bodies together. 

This app is especially well-suited for a mobile platform because it centers around location services, push notifications, and social sharing features. A mobile app is the best fit for this project since it is designed around real-time activity. For instance, workouts are scheduled in real locations and users need immediate access to geo-services— features that are difficult to access on the web. Additionally, mobile devices enable instant, timely engagement through push notifications, which are essential for reminding users about upcoming workouts and schedules. These real-time prompts are far more effective on a phone, which people carry throughout the day, in comparison to a web app that requires users to actively check a browser. Lastly, Align depends on quick photo capture and social sharing. Capturing and uploading photos on smartphones are much faster and easier than a web app.
